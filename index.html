Workshop on Social Robotics for Human-Centered Assistive and Rehabilitation AI

(a Fit4MedRob event)

Full-day workshop at ICSR 2025

Abstract

The workshop explores the integration of social robotics and artificial intelligence for human-centered assistive and rehabilitation solutions. With the increasing demand for personalized, adaptive, and continuous care, the event aims to bring together researchers and practitioners to discuss the latest advancements in AI-driven robotic platforms, user modeling, and adaptive interaction strategies. The focus is on bridging clinical, technological, and ethical perspectives to foster innovation in both clinical and home-care settings.

Topics of Interest

  • Personalisation in Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)
  • User modeling and adaptive robotics
  • Socially intelligent and affective robots
  • AI for rehabilitation and assistive robotics
  • Context and situation awareness
  • Personalised dialogue and non-verbal behaviour
  • Ethical, legal, and societal implications
  • Evaluation metrics and clinical impact

About Fit4MedRob

Fit4MedRob is a national project funded by the PNRR and coordinated by Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, aiming to enhance research and innovation in medical robotics. The initiative brings together multiple universities and research centers to develop intelligent robotic systems that support healthcare professionals and improve patient outcomes in rehabilitation, surgery, and diagnostics.
